Inspiration

Inspired by horses and their overpowering freedom Ceeyes Mobility Solutions has introduced Steed Chainless Bicycles, to provide a pleasant ride with pride.

Steed means Spirited Horse

Design

STEED Frame designs are validated through Finite Element Analysis to improve its overall performance by minimizing the mass of the frame and increasing its load carrying capacity.

STEED cycles frame, parts and sub assemblies are designed to meet various height, reach requirements for different age groups. Assembling is done virtually to ensure the fitness prior to manufacturing.
